## Security: What Happened in 2024 and What Changed

BingX had a hot wallet breach in September 2024 — somewhere between $43M and $52M in assets were compromised. Withdrawals were halted for under 24 hours, and the exchange reimbursed every affected user from its own reserves using what they called the "Shield Fund."

Since then, they rolled out a revamped security infrastructure called ShieldX — stronger firewall, real-time monitoring, cold storage for the majority of user funds. They also publish bi-weekly Proof of Reserves audits (previously conducted by Mazars) covering BTC, ETH, USDT, and USDC.

Does that make it perfectly safe? No exchange is. The sensible posture is the same everywhere: keep only what you're actively trading on-platform, move long-term holdings to self-custody.
